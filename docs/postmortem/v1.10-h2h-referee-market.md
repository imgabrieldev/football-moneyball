# Pitch — v1.10.0: H2H + Referee + Market Blending

## Problem

The current model (v1.9.0, 48 features, Brier 0.65) still doesn't use:

1. **Head-to-head history** — rivalries have clear patterns (e.g., Palmeiras has a historical advantage vs Botafogo). Zero H2H features.
2. **Referee tendencies** — Sofascore exposes them + we have 99.8% coverage (468/469 matches). Referees range from 2.8 to 5.3 cards/game. Affects red card probability and consequently goal probability.
3. **Market probs (Betfair/Pinnacle)** — Pinnacle is the efficient market reference. We have 20 matches with odds from 39 bookmakers. Operating a model without looking at the line = ignoring market information.

**Target:** Brier 0.65 → <0.58.

## Solution

### Part A: Head-to-Head Features (5 new features)

Computed from the last 5 matches between teams (in any competition):

- `h2h_home_win_rate` — % wins for home team in H2H
- `h2h_away_win_rate` — % wins for away team in H2H
- `h2h_draw_rate` — % draws
- `h2h_home_goals_avg` — average home goals
- `h2h_away_goals_avg` — average away goals

### Part B: Referee Features (3 new features)

Using referee_stats join via match_stats.referee_id:

- `ref_cards_per_game` — card tendency of the designated referee
- `ref_strictness` — quantile (0.0 lenient, 1.0 strict) in the distribution
- `ref_home_advantage` — home bias (cards_home/cards_away over the last 10)

### Part C: Market Blending (post-processing)

Not a feature — it's blending on the final output:

```
p_final = α · p_model + (1-α) · p_market_devigged
```

- Takes the closing line from Betfair or Pinnacle
- Removes vig (normalizes to sum to 1.0)
- Blend with α=0.55-0.70 (tuned via backtest)
